Donate

Your help is urgently needed! The London Animal Adoption Center relies on donations and fundraising efforts because we do not receive government funds or subsidies. A generous donation is to keep our center running. It maintains transparency by disclosing monthly donations and locations of use. Your donation helps and saves pets' lives. Donations can consist of various shapes and sizes. Learn more about it and help us!

Adopt

Adopt a pet and bring your new best friend home! Taking care of pets will give you a sense of purpose and a sense of accomplishment and reduce loneliness. We will help you find the perfect mate. You will be able to save lives, you will change the world of homeless animals. What's better than this? Learn more about adoption, cost, and available cats and dogs. I'm waiting for you!

Volunteer

We are looking for good people who are willing to open their homes and minds to help animals in need of special care before finding an eternal home. The program offers you a variety of opportunities to help animals in need. Our various volunteer activities provide opportunities to help animals in need, learn about proper care, and return them to the community in a meaningful way. We can't wait to welcome you to the volunteer team at the London Animal Adoption Center! If you have any questions or need help with volunteering, please contact us. Thank you.
