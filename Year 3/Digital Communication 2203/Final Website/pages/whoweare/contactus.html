Frequently asked questions - read questions before calling or emailing us

  • Adoption Process: If you fill out the adoption documents, we will do our best to find pets suitable for the environment. This process can take weeks or months. Please be patient!
  • Can I visit the London Animal Adoption Center? Because of COVID-19, no. Visitors cannot enter unless they make a reservation in advance. Please make an appointment by phone or email.
  • Do you accept food donations? What other items can you donate?: Yes, it is limited to the food that are not open yet. We also receive toys, pet beds, blankets and knit blankets (75cm x 75cm recommended size), towels, newspapers, shredded paper, etc. Your donation is a great boost to the London Animal Adoption Center!
  • Volunteer: Volunteering at the London Animal Adoption Center is incredibly rewarding. If you are 18 years of age or older, you can do volunteer work at our center. We are planning a youth volunteer program, so please check our volunteer page often. Our current volunteer opportunities can be found on the volunteer page. If you are interested in becoming a volunteer at the London Animal Adoption Center, visit our volunteer page.
